Welcome to Ministry of Health

Introduction The Ministry of Health

We are here to support your health and well-being. We strive to make a positive impact on your life, making sure that you get the care and support you need for a healthier life.

The Ministry of Health is a government agency dedicated to promoting the well-being of the population by ensuring the development and implementation of policies and programs concentrated on improving public health

Mission

The Ministry's Mission is to provide diverse healthcare services, improve healthcare systems and ensure that every individual has an oppertunity to access effective, affordable, and high-quality medical care.

Overview of Services

The Ministry of Health offers a wide variety of services, including;

  • Primary healthcare services
  • Disease prevention and health education
  • Public health awareness campaigns

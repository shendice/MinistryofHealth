Services Offered

Primary Healthcare Services

Primary Healthcare services are focus on providing comprehensive, accessible, and community-based care. It's mainly the first point of contact for persons looking for healthcare, making sure that continuous and affordable access to medical attention for people of all ages.

Disease Prevention and Health Education

Disease prevention and health education consists of strategies to protect persons and communities from infectious and chronic diseases through vaccination, screenings, and lifestyle.By raising awareness and encouraging preventive methods, ultimately improving overall community well-being.

Public Health Awareness Campaigns

Public health awareeness campaigns focus on raising awareness about topics such as primary healthcare services and disease prevention, health education. By using various media platforms, community help and educational programs.
